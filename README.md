# Dominos-Sales-2015-Analysis
# Objective: 
Analyzed pizza sales data to identify customer preferences and sales trends. Uncover insights into popular pizza ingredients, combinations, and types. Leverage data-driven insights to optimize business strategies and promotions. Enhance customer satisfaction and drive business growth.

# Dataset used:
Provided in file pizza_sales.csv

# Tools used:
Python and its plotly library - For data cleaning and visualization.

# Findings:
- Garlic, Tomatoes and Red Onions were the top 3 pizza ingredients people preferred the most.
- July was the most ordered month.
- Pepperoni, Mushrooms, Red Onions, Red peppers and Bacon pizza ingredients combination was the most ordered and preferred.
- Classic pizza had max contribution to sales.
- The classic deluxe pizza was preferred most.
- Larger quantities and bigger pizza sizes generally lead to higher total prices. 

# Conclusion:
To improve sales, businesses should focus on promoting pizzas with popular ingredients like garlic, tomatoes, and red onions, especially the classic deluxe variety. Targeting peak months like July with special offers could drive higher sales.
